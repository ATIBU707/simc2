Our Faith-Based Foundation

Sam Iga Memorial College is a church-founded institution committed to providing a holistic education grounded in strong moral and spiritual values. Offering both O-Level and A-Level education, the school fosters academic excellence while nurturing students' character, discipline, and social responsibility.

Moral Values

Strong ethical foundation and character development

Academic Excellence

Quality education and learning opportunities

Spiritual Growth

Faith-based guidance and support

Community Spirit

Supportive and inclusive environment

With a dedicated team of educators, a supportive learning environment, and a focus on both intellectual and personal growth, Sam Iga Memorial College prepares students to excel in their studies and become responsible citizens. Guided by its motto, 'God & Education Leads To success,' the school remains a beacon of quality education and faith-based learning.
