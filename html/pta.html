PTA Executives

Meet the Parent-Teacher Association leadership team

The Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) at Sam Iga Memorial College plays a vital role in fostering collaboration between parents and teachers to enhance the educational experience of our students. Our PTA executives work tirelessly to support the school's mission and vision.

Mr. John Ssewanyana

PTA Chairman

Mr. Ssewanyana has been actively involved in the PTA for over 5 years. He brings extensive experience in community leadership and is committed to strengthening the partnership between parents and teachers.
