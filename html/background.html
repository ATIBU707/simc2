Our Background

Discover the rich history and foundation of Sam Iga Memorial College

A Legacy of Excellence

Sam Iga Memorial College was established in [Year] with a vision to provide quality education grounded in strong moral and spiritual values. Named in honor of [Founder's Name], the school has grown from humble beginnings to become a leading educational institution in Uganda.

Our journey began with a small group of dedicated educators and a handful of students. Today, we proudly serve hundreds of students, offering both O-Level and A-Level education with a focus on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth.

The school's foundation is built on the principles of the Church of Uganda, integrating faith-based education with modern academic standards. This unique approach has helped shape generations of students who excel not only in their studies but also in their personal and spiritual development.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and spiritual growth, preparing students to become responsible citizens and future leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in their academic pursuits and personal lives.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, character, and personal development.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, ethical behavior, and moral principles in all our actions.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where students, staff, and families work together.

Sam Iga Memorial College is a Church of Uganda founded, Government aided "O" and "A" level, mixed day and boarding School.
