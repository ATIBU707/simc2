Tuition & Fees

Transparent fee structure for quality education

Fee Structure Overview

Our fees are structured to provide quality education while ensuring affordability. Fees are payable in three installments per term.

Payment Schedule

First installment: Beginning of term
Second installment: Mid-term
Third installment: End of term

Payment Methods

Bank transfer, Mobile money, Cash payment at the school accounts office
