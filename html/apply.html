How to Apply

Step-by-step guide to joining Sam Iga Memorial College

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in our supportive academic environment. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

1

Check Requirements

Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements for your desired level:

  • O-Level: Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) with at least 4 credits
  • A-Level: Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 credits
2

Gather Documents

Prepare the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Academic transcripts/certificates
  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Parent/Guardian ID
3

Submit Application

You can submit your application in one of two ways:

  • Online: Fill out the online application form below
  • In Person: Visit our admissions office during working hours
4

Interview & Assessment

After submitting your application:

  • You will be invited for an interview
  • Take a placement test to determine your academic level
  • Meet with our academic advisors
