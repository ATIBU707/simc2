O-Level Program

Comprehensive education for students in Senior 1-4

Our O-Level program provides a solid foundation for academic excellence and personal growth. We offer a balanced curriculum that prepares students for both the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations and future academic pursuits.

Program Overview

Duration

4 years (Senior 1-4)

Curriculum

Uganda National Curriculum

Entry Requirements

Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) with at least 4 credits

Exit Qualification

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)

Subjects Offered

Compulsory Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Religious Education

Optional Subjects

  • Agriculture
  • Commerce
  • Literature in English
  • Fine Art
  • Computer Studies
  • Food and Nutrition
  • Technical Drawing
  • Physical Education

Program Features

Experienced Teachers

Our O-Level teachers are highly qualified and experienced in their respective subjects.

Well-Equipped Labs

Modern science laboratories for practical experiments and hands-on learning.

Computer Facilities

Access to computer labs with internet connectivity for research and learning.

Library Resources

Extensive library with textbooks, reference materials, and study resources.

Performance Tracking

Regular assessments and progress reports to monitor student performance.

Small Class Sizes

Limited class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

Assessment & Evaluation

Our assessment system is comprehensive and includes:

  • Regular class tests and quizzes
  • Mid-term examinations
  • End-of-term examinations
  • Practical assessments for science subjects
  • Project work and assignments
  • Mock UCE examinations in Senior 4

Progress reports are issued at the end of each term, providing detailed feedback on academic performance, attendance, and conduct.
