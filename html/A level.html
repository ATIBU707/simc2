A-Level Program

Advanced education for students in Senior 5-6

Our A-Level program provides specialized education that prepares students for university and beyond. We offer a rigorous curriculum with a focus on critical thinking, research skills, and academic excellence.

Program Overview

Duration

2 years (Senior 5-6)

Curriculum

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE)

Entry Requirements

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 credits

Exit Qualification

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE)

Subject Combinations

Science Combinations

  • PCM: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • BCM: Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • PEM: Physics, Economics, Mathematics
  • BAG: Biology, Agriculture, Geography

Arts Combinations

  • HEG: History, Economics, Geography
  • LEG: Literature, Economics, Geography
  • HEA: History, Economics, Agriculture
  • HED: History, Economics, Divinity

General Requirements

  • General Paper (GP)
  • Subsidiary Mathematics
  • Subsidiary ICT

Program Features

Expert Faculty

Our A-Level teachers are highly qualified with advanced degrees and extensive teaching experience.

Advanced Laboratories

State-of-the-art science laboratories equipped for advanced experiments and research.

Research Facilities

Access to computer labs, internet resources, and research materials for academic projects.

Library Resources

Comprehensive library with textbooks, journals, and reference materials for advanced study.

Performance Monitoring

Regular assessments and detailed progress reports to track academic performance.

Small Class Sizes

Limited class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

Assessment & Evaluation

Our A-Level assessment system is comprehensive and includes:

  • Regular class tests and quizzes
  • Mid-term examinations
  • End-of-term examinations
  • Practical assessments for science subjects
  • Research projects and presentations
  • Mock UACE examinations in Senior 6

Progress reports are issued at the end of each term, providing detailed feedback on academic performance, attendance, and conduct.

University Preparation

Our A-Level program is designed to prepare students for university education. We provide:

  • Career guidance and counseling
  • University application assistance
  • Scholarship information and application support
  • Interview preparation for university admissions
  • Visits from university representatives
  • Alumni network connections
